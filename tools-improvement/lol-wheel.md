# LoL Champions Picker Wheel — Future Upgrades

**Route:** `/lol-wheel`  
**Competitor UGC:** ~150 League-related wheels

## What exists today

- Full champion dataset by role (top, jungle, mid, adc, support)
- Role filter, custom champions
- Display modes (emoji+name, etc.)
- Action modes: normal, elimination, manual
- Champion preview, results history
- AI tab, stats tab, champions tab
- Platform: achievements, themes, analytics, game modes
- Role spin statistics tracking
